COVID-19 Designated Hospitals are hospitals stipulated by the Chinese government to centrally treat patients with COVID-19. Click the layer button on the right side of the map to query the designated hospitals of different levels. Click a specific province to get a better view.

The choropleth map shows the sum of the maximum number of beds in designated hospitals in each province of China. The number of beds is the maximum capacity of the hospital, not refers to the beds' number for patients with COVID-19 treatment particularly.
